2. Origin & Formation

From quantum foam to blue giant 13.8Bya: Big Bang → H/He; multiple stellar generations; 4.6Bya: molecular cloud collapse. Frost line: Neptune formed beyond, T<50K, ices condensed. Formation timeline: dust condensation → planetesimal accretion (15 M⊕ core) → slow gas capture → migration possible. Triton capture destroyed original moons.

Stage 1 0-3 My: dust/ice condensation
Stage 2 3-30 My: 15 M⊕ core
Stage 3 30-100 My: thin H-He envelope
Stage 4-6 disk dispersal, migration, Triton capture

3. Composition

Atmosphere: H₂ ~80%, He ~19%, CH₄ 1-2% (blue color). "Ice giant" composition: 60-65% ices (H₂O, NH₃, CH₄), 25-30% rock, 10-15% H-He envelope. Deuterium ratio super‑primordial.

Core mass ~14 Earth masses. Ice mantle generates magnetic field.

5. Internal Heat

Neptune radiates 2.6× received sunlight — strongest relative output. Internal power ~2.2×10¹⁵ W. Theories: primordial heat, tidal heating from Triton, differentiation.

8. Density & 9. Atmosphere

Average density 1.64 g/cm³. Fastest winds: 2,100 km/h. Great Dark Spots (GDS-89, 2018 spot). Differential rotation: equator 18h, poles 12h. Bright clouds, methane escape at south pole.

11. Rotation & 12. Orbit

Axial tilt 28.3°. Sidereal (mag) 16.1h. Orbital period 165 years, eccentricity 0.0086. Since discovery (1846) only one full orbit completed.

14. Magnetic field

47° tilt, 55% offset from center. Surface field ~0.1-1.0 gauss. Generated in superionic water layer. Aurora in UV.

15. Rings & Arcs

6 narrow rings: Galle, LeVerrier, Lassell, Arago, Adams (with arcs Liberté, Égalité, Fraternité). Shepherd moons (Galatea). Dark, dusty.

16. Moons

Triton (retrograde, 2707 km, geysers, captured KBO). Nereid (eccentric), 7 inner moons (Proteus, Hippocamp…), 5 irregular outer moons. Neptune Trojans (22 known).

Triton: -235°C, N₂ atmosphere
Nereid: 340 km, e=0.75
Proteus: 2nd largest
Neso: 27yr orbit

17-22. Life? black hole? star?

Life unlikely on Neptune; Triton may have subsurface ocean. Black hole impossible (Schwarzschild radius 15cm). Needs 235× mass for deuterium fusion (brown dwarf) or 1412× for star. Atomic/quantum: superionic water, electron degeneracy.

23. Large-scale

Mass 1×10²⁶ kg; surface area 7.62b km²; atoms ~10⁵². Walking around equator = 8.5 years.
